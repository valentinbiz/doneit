How to use the Pomodoro Timer?

×
This project combines 2 different productivity tools, the Kanban Board and the Pomodoro technique. They can be used together or individually, depending on your preferences.

1. Edit the duration of the sessions and the behaviour of the pomodoro app in the settings

2. Start Pomodoro focus session

3. When the pomodoro session finishes, take a break

4. In case you select the sessions to start automatically, a long break will start after 4 Pomodoro sessions

If you are not familiar with the Kanban Board, just follow the instructions displayed in the columns

Optional: listen to some music to help you focus or absorb some stoic wisdom
